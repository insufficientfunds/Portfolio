Element Selection

This course seeks to accomplish many things. It's foundation and ultimate course objective is to emphasize concepts, architectures, componenets, protocols and standards for data exchange within networks.

In turn, this will spur on a student's knowledge of data communications and networking fundamentals, the understanding of various communication protocols, a better understanding of hardware and software applications and its relation to facilitate entriprise goals.
